About Onyx Gas

Onyx Gas is a trusted provider of professional plumbing and gas safety services across Wembley and surrounding areas. With over 10 years of experience, we combine technical expertise with exceptional customer service.

Our team is fully Gas Safe registered and trained to the highest industry standards. We understand that gas safety is paramount, which is why we adhere strictly to IGEM/UP/1B standards and GIUSP compliance protocols on every job.

Whether you need a new boiler installation, annual servicing, or an emergency repair, we're here to help 24/7.
